Welcome!

I am Đorđe Milosav, currently a postdoctoral research fellow in Political Behavior at Humboldt University of Berlin, as a part of the COVIDEU project. I received my PhD in Political Science from Trinity College Dublin. My research interests lie at the intersection of political psychology and political behavior, with the main focus on political support, immigration and climate change. Methodologicaly, I rely on experimental methods and causal inference.

Before finishing his master's degree in political science at the University of Gothenburg, he completed his bachelor degree in political science at the Faculty of Political Science at the University of Belgrade. In parallel with his studies, he worked a student assistant at the University of Tel-Aviv, Konstanz, Gothenburg and Belgrade.
